## Email Generation Prompt (Used with Gemini)

```
You are writing a personalized business outreach email for Arc UI, 
a web development service.

Business Details:
- Business Name: {business_name}
- City: {city}
- Industry: {category}
- Website: {website}

{website_analysis_findings}

Write a professional, personalized cold email that:
1. Addresses the business by name and mentions their city
2. References what they do (their industry/service)
3. If website observations are provided, briefly mention 1-2 specific 
   observations (keep it subtle and helpful, not critical)
4. Introduces Arc UI (https://arc-ui.vercel.app/) as a full-service 
   web solution
5. Highlights the offer: $99/month for website, hosting, updates, and 
   maintenance - "we handle everything"
6. Keeps a friendly, helpful tone (not salesy)
7. Is concise (under 150 words)
8. Ends with a soft call-to-action
9. Includes an unsubscribe line at the bottom

Format your response as:
SUBJECT: [email subject line]

BODY:
[email body]

Keep the email personalized but professional. Don't be too pushy.
```

## Personalization Variables

The AI writer receives these variables for each business:
- `business_name`: Company name
- `city`: Location
- `category`: Business type/industry
- `website`: URL (if available)
- `site_analysis`: JSON with issues and observations

## Email Best Practices Applied

1. **Personalization**: Every email mentions specific business name and city
2. **Relevance**: References the actual industry/service
3. **Value Proposition**: Clear $99/month offer with specific benefits
4. **Social Proof**: Mentions other businesses in same city/industry
5. **Soft CTA**: Question format, not demanding
6. **Compliance**: Unsubscribe option in every email
7. **Professional Tone**: Helpful, not pushy
8. **Brevity**: Under 150 words for better response rates
9. **Authentic**: References real website observations
10. **Clear Branding**: Arc UI identity with link

## Response Tracking

While the bot doesn't track responses, businesses can:
- Reply directly to the email
- Click the Arc UI link
- Request to unsubscribe

All responses go to the configured `REPLY_TO_EMAIL` address.
